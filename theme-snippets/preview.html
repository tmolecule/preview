Overview

Spice Rush is a revitalizing black tea blend built on four generations of tea-sourcing know-how. We take bold, full-bodied black tea, mill it to a fine powder for a smoother brew, and blend in collagen peptides and three warming spices: ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom.

One scoop, hot water, stir — thirty seconds to a full cup. No steeping, no straining, no timer. Drink it black, or add milk and a touch of sweetener to taste. A cleaner energy than coffee, a warmer ritual than a supplement.

Tasting Notes

Bold and malty up front, warming ginger mid-palate, sweet cinnamon and floral cardamom on the finish. Rich body from milling. Drinks beautifully black, or with a splash of milk and a pinch of salt for a creamier chai.

Brewing

Hot: Add one scoop (≈5g) to 8oz near-boiling water (200–212°F). Stir 15 seconds. That's it.

Iced: Mix one scoop with 2oz hot water and stir to suspend, then pour over ice and top with cold water or milk.

Latte: Stir into 6oz steamed milk (dairy or oat) with a teaspoon of honey.

FAQs
How much caffeine is in a scoop?

About 40–50mg per scoop — roughly half a cup of coffee. Sustained energy, no jitters.

What kind of collagen do you use?

Hydrolyzed bovine collagen peptides. Unflavored, and heat-stable — the peptides stay intact in hot water, so the benefits come through in every cup.

Is this keto and paleo friendly?

Yes — zero sugar, zero starch, zero fillers. Just five ingredients: black tea, collagen peptides, ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom.

Why milled instead of loose leaf?

Milling creates a homogenous blend with the spices and collagen so every scoop delivers the same balanced cup. No steeping, no strainer, no bitter over-brew.

How long does a pack last?

Each pack contains roughly 20 servings. At one cup a day, that's about three weeks.

How should I store it?

Keep the pack sealed at room temperature, away from direct sunlight and humidity. Use by the expiration date printed on the package.
